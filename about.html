ABOUT US

CASHONHEELS

A one stop financial solution for nursing mothers and moms to be

HISTORY

The decision to establish this for-profit organisation was borne out of a burden to encourage nursing mothers and moms to be who at the long run have financial constraints due to their inability to retain their jobs bridge this gap with digital literacy.

VISION

To become a one stop financial solution for nursing mothers and moms to be

MISSION

  • To create an enabling environment for educating nursing mothers in digital cash generating activities
  • To empower moms to be and nursing mothers in planning and making provisions for the children
  • To create continuous awareness through collaborations on the importance of maximizing the digital space
